Who We Are

A Family Farm Built on Trust & Quality

PMV Goat Farm is a dedicated goat breeding farm located in Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu. We specialise in raising healthy, premium-quality Ettayapuram Mayilambadi and Andhra Nellore Jodipi breeds.

Our goats are raised in a clean, natural environment with proper nutrition and veterinary care. We believe in transparent, honest dealings — every goat we sell is healthy, vaccinated, and well-documented.

  • ✅ Vaccinated & Health-Checked
  • ✅ Natural Free-Range Environment
  • ✅ Trusted by 200+ Farmers
  • ✅ Direct Farm-to-Buyer Sales

What We Offer

Our Premium Goat Breeds

We raise two of the most sought-after goat breeds in Tamil Nadu, known for their quality meat and adaptability.

Ettayapuram Mayilambadi

📍 Tamil Nadu Native Breed

A hardy, indigenous breed known for excellent meat quality and adaptability to local climate conditions.

  • High meat yield
  • Drought-resistant
  • Easy to maintain
  • Strong immune system
